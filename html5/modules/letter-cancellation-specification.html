Letter Cancellation Task
Description:
A large field of letters is displayed, including CAPITAL LETTERS, double spaces.


Page 1 is presented for 60 seconds (30 secs for practice)
Capital letters should be marked.
Page 2 is presented for 60 seconds (Excluded in practice run)
Capital letters should be marked.
Page 3 is presented for 60 seconds (30 secs for practice)
Double space should be marked, with a mark before and after the space.
Page 4 is presented for 60 seconds (Excluded in practice run)
Double space should be marked, with a mark before and after the space.
Page 5 is presented for 60 seconds (30 secs for practice)
Capital letters and double space should be marked.
Page 6 is presented for 60 seconds (Excluded in practice run)
Capital letters and double space should be marked.
Page 7 is presented until stop button is pressed (Half page in practice run)


Capital letters and double space should be marked.
Marking is a thin red cross on top of
Remove the cross if when same location clicked a second time
See Flash Code for the letters on each page.
Font used is Lucida Console and font size 12
In the original LCT there are extra empty pages with only a "next" button. Please skip these.

Researchers Specs
Flash Code
Instruction pages:
Page 1:
Capital Letters Condition
This task is called a cancellation task.
Look for the CAPITAL LETTERS on each line.
Mark each CAPITAL LETTER with the left button on the mouse
Go from left to right working as quickly as you can
If you make a mistake you can re-click on the letter to remove the cross.


Page 2:
Capital Letters Condition Second Page
This task is called a cancellation task.
Look for the CAPITAL LETTERS on each line.
Mark each CAPITAL LETTER with the left button on the mouse
Go from left to right working as quickly as you can
If you make a mistake you can re-click on the letter to remove the cross.

Page 3:
Spaces Condition
This time - look for the D O U B L E S P A C E S.
Mark the letters before AND after the double space with the left button on the mouse.
Go from left to right working as quickly as you can.
Try not to miss any.
If you make a mistake you can re-click on the letter to remove the cross.


Page 4:
Spaces Condition Second Page
This time - look for the D O U B L E S P A C E S.
Mark the letters before AND after the double space with the left button on the mouse.
Go from left to right working as quickly as you can.
Try not to miss any.
If you make a mistake you can re-click on the letter to remove the cross.

Page 5:
Dual Condition
This time you have both tasks to do at once
Mark the CAPITAL LETTERS and the letters before AND after the DOUBLE SPACE with the left button on the mouse.
Go from left to right working as quickly as you can.
Try not to miss any.
If you make a mistake you can re-click on the letter to remove the cross.


Page 6:
Dual Condition Second Page
This time you have both tasks to do at once
Mark the CAPITAL LETTERS and the letters before AND after the DOUBLE SPACE with the left button on the mouse.
Go from left to right working as quickly as you can.
Try not to miss any.
If you make a mistake you can re-click on the letter to remove the cross.

Page 7:
Processing Speed Condition
In this final task -
Mark both the CAPITAL LETTERS and the letters before and after the DOUBLE SPACES with the left button on the mouse.
This time complete the entire page as quickly and accurately as possible.
Go from left to right as quickly as you can without missing any.
If you make a mistake you can re-click on the letter to remove the cross.
When you finish click 'Stop'


Returned data for every page:
Time the page was present (Page 1 to 6 fixed time, Page 7 - Measure time until Stop button is pressed)
Number of correct markings
Number of missed markings
Number of wrong markings
